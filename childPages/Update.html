      June 2022 Summer Update

        1. Balance changes

              Clash of Clans' professional developer team always works hard to keep the game balanced as new things are added to the game from time to time.

          Here are some significant changes in the recent update:
  1. Super Valkyrie's attack speed was increased ↑ by 0.7s and she also received a small buff in rage radius, but her movement speed was decreased ↓ by 0.5 tiles.
  2. HP of Level 9 - 14 Walls has been decreased ↓ by 500-2000
  3. Flame Flingers hit radius has been reduced, fire damage duration was increased ↑ by 50% but the max DPS was decreased ↓ by 33%.

        2. Quality of life changes

              As more gamers discover this game, Supercell puts more effort in improving the overall gaming experience of people. The recent update introduces new                 features like free training, super troop quick donation, "remove all" function in army training etc.

        3. Game Changes

              Clash of Clans' game devs care about their players a lot, take the Clan Capital changes as an example. The changes like making the "Sold Out" notice more                 visible to players, friendly district challenges, a new added button to view the enemy clan, specific notifications etc. are all based on community posts or                     comments in social media platforms. Also, they chose to remove country flag decorations due to gaming experience of Clashers (see below).
