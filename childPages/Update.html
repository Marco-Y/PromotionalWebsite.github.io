      June 2022 Summer Update

        1. Balance changes

             Stats of numerous troops, buildings and spells have changed.

          Here are some significant changes:

             i. Super Valkyrie's attack speed was increased by 0.7s and she also received a small buff in rage radius, but her movement speed was decreased by 0.5 tiles.
             ii. Level 9 - 14 Walls' HP decreased by 500              iii. Flame Flingers hit radius has been reduced, fire damage duration was increased by 50% but the max DPS was decreased by 33%
