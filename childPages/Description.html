Gameplay

Clash of Clans, invented by Supercell, is an online real-time strategy mobile game while offering single-player mode for players. Players form clans, train troops and raid enemy villages to earn resources, which will be used to build and upgrade stuff. It might seem easy to play but it's actually extremely hard to master, thus Clashers who achieved highest rank of the game are called legends. So what are you waiting for? Join now and battle your way to the top!

Achievements

[Source from Wikipedia]

Below are some of the greatest achievements of Clash of Clans

  • 2012/12 - 2013/05 App Store Top 5 Downloaded App
  • 2015 Top Grossing App in both Google Play and App Store with an estimated revenue of 1.5Mil/day
  • $6.4b revenue from 2012-2018
  • Amassed 500 million downloads by 2018
  • The success of Clash of Clans has been described as helping to usher in a new era in single-player gaming on mobile devices.
