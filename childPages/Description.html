Gameplay

Clash of Clans, invented by Supercell, is an online real-time strategy mobile game while offering single-player mode for players. Players form clans, train troops and raid enemy villages to earn resources, which will be used to build and upgrade stuff. Although it might seem easy, it is actually extremely hard to master, thus people in the highest rank of the game are called legends. So what are you waiting for? Join now and become a legend!
