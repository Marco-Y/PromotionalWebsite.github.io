Gameplay

Clash of Clans, invented by Supercell, is an online real-time strategy mobile game while offering single-player mode for players. Players form clans, train troops and raid enemy villages to earn resources, which will be used to build and upgrade stuff. Although it might seem easy, it is actually extremely hard to master, thus the title of Clashers in the highest rank of the game is legend. So what are you waiting for? Join now and become a legend!

Achievements

Below are some of the greatest achievements of Clash of Clans

  • 2012/12 - 2013/05 App Store Top 5 Downloaded App
  • 2015 Top Grossing App in both Google Play and App Store with an estimated revenue of 1.5Mil/day
  • $6.4b revenue from 2012-2018
  • Amassed 500 million downloads by 2018

Ratings

Below are Clash of Clans' ratings from players

  • Received a 4.6 and 4.7 star rating in Google Play and App Store respectively, which is relatively high compared to the average star rating of 4.05 and 4.53 in Google Play and App Store respectively [source]
  • Received overall positive ratings from the community and websites like Pocket Gamer, Gamezebo etc.
  • Pocket Gamer's Peter Wellington scored the game 9/10 and gave it a "Gold Award". He concluded that "Clash of Clans is a superb game, freemium or otherwise, with more nuance than most give it credit for. That's why it's passed the test of time since its launch and still has an active community devotedly constructing elaborate fortresses in the hope of becoming invincible." [source]
